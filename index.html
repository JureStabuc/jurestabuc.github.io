About me

I am a data journalist currently completing the MSc course in Computational and Data journalism at Cardiff University. In the past year Iâ€™ve been learning how to make use of coding languages in journalism by scraping data, finding stories in large datasets, visualising it and producing news apps. I got interested in news as a child, some say a bit too early. But it piqued my interest and got me pondering upon the thrill of being the one responsible for conveying exciting stories to the public.
